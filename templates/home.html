RVPS: Reactive Vehicle Positioning System

Big Data and Machine Learning

The RVPS uses 260,000 data points with machine learning to produce the RVPS algorithms.

Deaths in shooting incidents often happen when a victim is not given the proper treatment in time; death can occur anywhere from seconds to minutes of bleeding out, making it crucial that ambulance stations are optimized around the city to ensure that they reach the scene promptly. In this scenario, an extra few seconds could be the difference between potential resuscitation and death.


Ambulance Location Optimizer

RVPS, through this application, provides emergency service providers with optimal location positioning information.

Our incomplete dataset alone reports 60,468 killings and 118,402 injuries between the five year period the data was collected. Law enforcement and emergency medical services could radically decrease their response time if they could use a proactive approach in stationing their operational vehicles. This application of the PVPS would suggest the optimal location the emergency services providers should station their vehicles based off of the following inputs: month of the year, day of the week, and number of vehicles available.
